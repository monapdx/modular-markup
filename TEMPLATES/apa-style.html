APA Citation Style Guide (7th Edition)

Source Type Reference List Format In-Text Citation Key Conventions
Book Author, A. A. (Year). Title of the book. Publisher. (Author, Year)
Author (Year)		 Sentence case title.
Italicize book title.
No publisher location.
Journal Article Author, A. A. (Year). Title of article. Journal Title, Volume(Issue), xx–xx. (Author, Year) Journal in title case.
Volume italicized.
Include DOI if available.
Webpage Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). Title of page. Website Name. URL (Author, Year) Include full date.
No period after URL.
Chapter in Edited Book Author. (Year). Chapter title. In Editor (Ed.), Book Title (pp. xx–xx). (Author, Year) Chapter not italicized.
Book title italicized.
3+ Authors Author, A., Author, B., & Author, C. (Year)... (Author et al., Year) Use et al. in-text.
Direct Quote Same as source (Author, Year, p. 23) Include page number.
Use p. or pp.
No Author Title of Work. (Year). (Short Title, Year) Title moves to author position.
No Date Author. (n.d.). Title. (Author, n.d.) Use n.d.
Multiple Sources Separate entries (Author, 2020; Other, 2021) Alphabetical order.
Separate with semicolons.
